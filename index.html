Controls

- Enter the playlist id for the playlist you would like to shuffle, then hit "Randomize". It'll take a few seconds to load the playlist.

- Don't like the order? Hit "Reshuffle" to change the order again.


- If you have additional playlists you want to play, you can enter the id below and hit "Append". The playlist will automatically be reshuffled. Read "Important Information" for more info.

Important Information

Only the first 100 videos of a given playlist will be generated. This means if you want to shuffle a playlist with more, you'll need to split the playlist into 100 video chunks and append them. Youtube doesn't currently have a built in way to split playlists, but copying a playlist into a new one and deleting the first 100 videos will do the trick. This is of course tedious, but only needs to be done once.

FAQ

What is a playlist ID? How can I find it?

Youtube gives every playlist a unique ID upon creation. To find it, navigate to the youtube page of your playlist. At the end of the url, you should see "list=[playlistID]".

Why does it tell me there are no videos in the playlist?

It's likely that you entered an invalid playlist id; copy paste the id from your playlist page and try again. Also, keep in mind that certain playlists are not accessible. YouTube auto-generated playlists and private playlists cannot be played.

It keeps telling me the server connection failed. How do I fix this?

Most often, this happens when the playlist has a private video in it. Private videos cannot be accessed, so the request will get blocked and the connection will fail. The easiest fix is to simply copy the playlist into a fresh one and delete the private videos.

I want to skip to a certain video in the playlist. How can I do this?

Find which video you want to skip to in the video list, and click on it.

How can I search for a specific video?

CTRL+F (or equivalent). Then enter the title of the video you want.

What if I mix playlists with duplicate videos?

All duplicate videos are filtered out by default.

What happens when the playlist ends?

The website will automatically reshuffle the current playlist and start from the beginning. If you want to play a new playlist, you'll have to enter the new id and hit "Randomize".
