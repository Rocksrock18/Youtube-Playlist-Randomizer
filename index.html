FAQ

How can I access the playlist url?

Just navigate to the youtube page of your playlist. The url should look something like .

Will the site remember where I was previously?

Yes! This website will store your most recent playlist and where you were in that playlist. Hitting "Resume" will load your stored information.

How big can the playlist be?

This tool can handle playlists of any size. It may just take a bit longer to load initially.

Why does it tell me there are no videos in the playlist?

It's likely that you entered an invalid playlist url; copy paste the url from your playlist page and try again. Also, keep in mind that certain playlists are not accessible. YouTube auto-generated playlists and private playlists cannot be played.

I want to skip to a certain video in the playlist. How can I do this?

Find which video you want to skip to in the video list, and click on it.

How can I search for a specific video?

CTRL+F (or equivalent). Then enter the title of the video you want.

What if I mix playlists with duplicate videos?

All duplicate videos are filtered out by default.

What happens when the playlist ends?

The website will automatically reshuffle the current playlist and start from the beginning. If you want to play a new playlist, you'll have to enter the new id and hit "Randomize".
